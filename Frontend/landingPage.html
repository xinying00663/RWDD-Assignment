FAQ

How can I start recycling?

Begin by separating paper, plastics, and glass at home. Check your local collection rules.

What are some easy energy-saving tips?

Use LED bulbs, unplug devices when not in use, and consider energy-efficient appliances.

Do I need a big garden to grow food?

No, you can grow food in small spaces using containers or vertical gardening techniques.
