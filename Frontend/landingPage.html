FAQ

What is EcoGo?

EcoGo is a simple initiative to inspire small, sustainable actions in daily life.

How can I start recycling?

Begin by separating paper, plastics, and glass at home. Check your local collection rules.

Do I need a big garden to grow food?

No. Start with herbs or small vegetables in containers on a balcony or windowsill.
